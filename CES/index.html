FAQ

  • Q1. Can I make changes to my
    application ?
    You cannot change your application once it's
    submitted.
  • Q2. Will there be any disadvantages to
    not submitting a video?
    A video self-introduction as well a video clip for
    your audition category are mandatory; to
    ensure a fair review process, you must submit
    all required materials to be eligible for
    consideration.
  • Q3. Will there be in-person open
    auditions?
    There are currently no plans for in-person open
    auditions, and only online open auditions will
    be held. We will provide a separate notice on
    our website if in-person auditions are planned
    for the future.
  • Q4. When will the results of the
    audition be announced?
    Review will take about 2 to 4 weeks, and individuals who passed will be contacted
    separately.
